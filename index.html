Introduction

This report outlines a detailed plan to achieve a yearly target of 12,000 cedis through referrals and installations of vehicle trackers. The plan focuses on both tricycles and cars, with an emphasis on tricycles due to my current expertise. The calculations are based on the following commission structure:

  • Tricycles:
    • Referral only: 50 cedis
    • Referral + installation: 90 cedis
  • Cars:
    • Referral only: 120 cedis
    • Referral + installation: 180 cedis

Assumptions

To ensure the target is achievable, the following assumptions were made:

  1. Focus on Tricycles: 80% of the target will come from tricycles, as they are my area of expertise.
  2. Include Cars: 20% of the target will come from cars, with a modest goal of 1 car per month.
  3. Referral + Installation: All calculations assume that both referral and installation services are provided, as this maximizes earnings.

Target Breakdown

The yearly target of 12,000 cedis is broken down as follows:

1. Tricycles (80% of Target)

  • Yearly Target: 9,600 cedis
  • Commission per Tricycle: 90 cedis (referral + installation)
  • Number of Tricycles Required:
    • Yearly: 9,600 ÷ 90 = 107 tricycles
    • Monthly: 107 ÷ 12 = 9 tricycles
    • Weekly: 9 ÷ 4 = 2 tricycles

2. Cars (20% of Target)

  • Yearly Target: 2,400 cedis
  • Commission per Car: 180 cedis (referral + installation)
  • Number of Cars Required:
    • Yearly: 2,400 ÷ 180 = 13 cars
    • Monthly: 13 ÷ 12 = 1 car
    • Weekly: 1 ÷ 4 = 0.25 cars (1 car every 4 weeks)

Monthly and Weekly Goals

To achieve the yearly target, the following monthly and weekly goals are recommended:

Vehicle Type Monthly Goal Weekly Goal Commission
Tricycles 9 tricycles 2 tricycles 90 cedis each
Cars 1 car 0.25 cars 180 cedis each

Projected Earnings

Based on the above goals, the projected earnings are as follows:

1. Tricycles:

  • Monthly Earnings: 9 × 90 = 810 cedis
  • Yearly Earnings: 107 × 90 = 9,630 cedis

2. Cars:

  • Monthly Earnings: 1 × 180 = 180 cedis
  • Yearly Earnings: 13 × 180 = 2,340 cedis

Total Earnings:

  • Yearly Total: 9,630 + 2,340 = 11,970 cedis
  • Monthly Total: 810 + 180 = 990 cedis
  • Weekly Total: 247.5 cedis

Action Plan

To achieve the target, the following steps will be taken:

  1. Focus on Tricycles: Aim to complete 2 tricycles per week (referral + installation).
  2. Include Cars Occasionally: Aim to complete 1 car every 4 weeks (referral + installation).
  3. Track Progress: Use a spreadsheet or tracking tool to monitor my weekly and monthly progress.
  4. Exceed Targets Where Possible: If opportunities arise, exceed my weekly or monthly goals to build a buffer and ensure the yearly target is met.

Conclusion

By focusing on 107 tricycles and 13 cars over the course of a year, it is possible to achieve my target of 12,000 cedis. This plan is designed to be realistic and achievable, with a focus on maximizing earnings through referrals and installations.
