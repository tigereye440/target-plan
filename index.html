Introduction

This report outlines a detailed plan to achieve a yearly target of 12,000 cedis through referrals and installations of vehicle trackers. The plan focuses on both tricycles and cars, with an emphasis on tricycles due to my current expertise. The calculations are based on the following commission structure:

  • Tricycles:
    • Referral only: 50 cedis
    • Referral + installation: 50 + 30 = 80 cedis
  • Cars:
    • Referral only: 120 cedis
    • Referral + installation: 120 + 50 = 170 cedis

Assumptions

To ensure the target is achievable, the following assumptions were made:

  1. Focus on Tricycles: 80% of the target will come from tricycles, as they are my area of expertise.
  2. Include Cars: 20% of the target will come from cars, with a modest goal of 1 car per month.
  3. Referral + Installation: All calculations assume that both referral and installation services are provided, as this maximizes earnings.

Target Breakdown

The yearly target of 15,000 cedis is broken down as follows:

1. Tricycles (80% of Target)

  • Yearly Target: 12,000 cedis
  • Commission per Tricycle: 80 cedis (referral + installation)
  • Number of Tricycles Required:
    • Yearly: 12,000 ÷ 80 = 150 tricycles
    • Monthly: 150 ÷ 12 = 12.5 tricycles
    • Weekly: 12.5 ÷ 4 = 3.125 tricycles

2. Cars (20% of Target)

  • Yearly Target: 3,000 cedis
  • Commission per Car: 170 cedis (referral + installation)
  • Number of Cars Required:
    • Yearly: 3,000 ÷ 170 = 17.65 cars
    • Monthly: 17.65 ÷ 12 = 1.47 cars
    • Weekly: 1.47 ÷ 4 = 0.37 cars
    • 0.37 cars (1 car every 3-4 weeks)

Monthly and Weekly Goals

To achieve the yearly target, the following monthly and weekly goals are recommended:

Vehicle Type Monthly Goal Weekly Goal Commission
Tricycles 12.5 tricycles 3.125 tricycles 80 cedis each
Cars 1.47 cars 0.37 cars 170 cedis each

Projected Earnings

Based on the above goals, the projected earnings are as follows:

1. Tricycles:

  • Monthly Earnings: 12.5 × 80 = 1000 cedis
  • Yearly Earnings: 150 × 80 = 12,000 cedis

2. Cars:

  • Monthly Earnings: 1.47 × 170 = 250 cedis
  • Yearly Earnings: 17.65 × 170 = 3000 cedis

Total Earnings:

  • Yearly Total: 12000 + 3000 = 15000 cedis
  • Monthly Total: 1000 + 250 = 1,250 cedis
  • Weekly Total: 313 cedis

Action Plan

To achieve the target, the following steps are recommended:

  1. Focus on Tricycles: Aim to complete 2.5 tricycles per week (referral + installation).
  2. Include Cars Occasionally: Aim to complete 1 car every 3-4 weeks (referral + installation).
  3. Track Progress: Use a spreadsheet or tracking tool to monitor my weekly and monthly progress.
  4. Exceed Targets Where Possible: If opportunities arise, exceed the weekly or monthly goals to build a buffer and ensure the yearly target is met.

Conclusion

By focusing on 150 tricycles and 17.65 cars over the course of a year, it is possible to achieve the target of 15,000 cedis. This plan is designed to be realistic and achievable, with a focus on maximizing earnings through referrals and installations. Regular tracking and adjustments will ensure success.
